INDUSTRIAL SOLUTIONS

Elevators for urban life

Rocket Elevators' solutions provide access for urban live. We help owners and builders add value to any building type.

Our elevators, technologies and services are recognized as a benchmark for quality, safety and style improving mobility in buildings.

Rocket elevators efficiently move passengers in notable buildings, including hospitals, hotels, offices, residences, airports, arenas and sports facilities. All elevators are designed to meet governing safety codes and they also comply with the Disabilities Act.

Standard

The key features of the Rocket Elevator 900+ are traffic performance, reduced time to destination, greater architectural flexibility, and optimized usable building space.

The Rocket 900+ can also provide individualization for passengers and their needs, multi-level access control, as well as visitor control and guidance – all reconfigurable by the customer at any time.

Superior traction technology provides passengers with a noticeably more comfortable and quieter ride and Eco-friendly features lower operating costs.

Premium

The Rocket 8000+ modernization solution can completely transform your elevators. This total system upgrade package delivers the best of today’s elevator technology quickly and affordably.

Enjoy the faster, quieter and smoother ride. The new developments in traction media and control systems give you not only more speed, but also lets you handle more passengers and goods while providing optimal ride quality.

Rely on the latest technology with up to 30% energy savings. The Rocket 8000+ helps you lower your building’s carbon footprint by using energy more efficiently – with regenerative drives for sustainable operation.

Excelium

Rocket’s service delivery model will keep your elevators in optimal running condition. Rocket’s destination dispatching system can be linked to your building management system for even better control. Our dense service network guarantees quick response if needed and a Rocket elevators technician is always nearby.

Rocket 9500+ does not need a separate machine room thanks to the Rocket hoisting motor, which is so compact that it can be located in the hoistway. There is no need for a control room as the control and logic components fit inside the wall of the top elevator landing.
