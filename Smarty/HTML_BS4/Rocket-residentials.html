RESIDENTIALS SOLUTIONS

Elevators for multi-family buildings

Rocket Elevators' safe solutions help move residents through your building at all times of the day. Our productos minimize wait time, provide reliable transportation and a comfortable ride experience to enhance your residents' quality of life.

Rocket Elevetors offers innovative and eco-efficient passenger and goods elevators for all types of buildings, from low and mid-rise structures to the world’s tallest skyscrapers.

Standard

Thanks to innovative Rocket 900 manufacturing and installation, your new elevator can be tailored to fit your building’s existing hoistway and entrances. Plus, you’ll gain back extra building space with machine room-less design and an optional Integrated Control System (ICS) that fits inside your elevator’s top landing entrance.

Competitively-priced machine room-less traction elevator that outperforms hydraulic alternatives.

Superior traction technology provides passengers with a noticeably more comfortable and quieter ride and Eco-friendly features lower operating costs.

Premium

The Rocket 8000 brings the benefits of gearless technology and Rocket's suspension traction media. The suspension and traction requires less space than traditional cables and offer a smoother, reliable ride.

The Rocket 8000 modernization solution can completely transform your elevators. This total system upgrade package delivers the best of today’s elevator technology quickly and affordably.

Modernizing the controller and electrification system will improve its reliability, eco-efficiency, safety, and accessibility.Because our electrification solutions are quick to install, there will be minimal disruption for building residents.

Excelium

Rocket 9500 is the modular passenger elevator that combines performance, flexibility and design, which makes it easy to fit to the requirements mid and high-end residential buildings.

Elevate your building with tall cars, entrances and center opening doors. All this sophistication with simple, quick and efficient ordering to installation processes.

Quieter, smoother traction technology combined with expanded service capacity and range. Passengers will notice the better ride. Building owners will appreciate the increased capabilities.
